# Doxxy Healthcare Platform - Feature Overview

## 🏥 **Complete Practice Management Solution**

Doxxy is a modern, multi-tenant healthcare practice management platform that combines cutting-edge technology with deep clinical expertise to streamline clinic operations and enhance patient care.

---

## 🎯 **Core Capabilities**

### **🏢 Multi-Clinic Architecture**
- **True Multi-Tenancy**: Secure clinic-level data isolation
- **Clinic Creation**: Streamlined setup with role-based access
- **Department Management**: Specialized clinical departments
- **Staff Management**: Unlimited doctors and staff members
- **Clinic Switching**: Seamless navigation between clinics

### **👥 Patient Management**
- **Complete Patient Profiles**: Demographic & medical information
- **Advanced Search**: Quick patient lookup and filtering
- **Medical Timeline**: Visual history of visits and treatments
- **Medical Records**: Comprehensive health record management

### **📅 Appointment Management**
- **Smart Scheduling**: Book appointments with doctors
- **Status Tracking**: Scheduled → In Progress → Completed workflow
- **Calendar Views**: Weekly appointment charts and dashboards
- **Digital vs Walk-in**: Support for both consultation types
