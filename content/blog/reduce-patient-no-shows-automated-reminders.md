The industry average for no-shows in outpatient clinics is 23%. For some specialties, it's closer to 30%. That means on a day with 30 scheduled appointments, 7 to 9 patients simply don't arrive — and most clinics don't find out until the empty chair stares back at them.

Those empty slots represent lost revenue, wasted provider time, and other patients who could have been seen but couldn't get an appointment. The good news: clinics using automated reminders cut no-shows by 40%. The better news: it's not hard to set up.

## Why Patients Don't Show Up

The most common reason isn't dissatisfaction or finding another doctor. It's forgetfulness. A patient booked an appointment three weeks ago. Life happened. They forgot.

The second most common reason: inconvenience. The slot they booked no longer works for their schedule. They need to reschedule but don't want to call, wait on hold, and explain. So they just don't show up.

Both problems have the same solution: automated communication that reaches patients where they already are.

## SMS and WhatsApp: The Channels That Actually Work

Email reminders have a 20% open rate on a good day. Phone calls require staff time and often go unanswered. SMS and WhatsApp messages have open rates above 90% — and they're read within minutes.

Doxxy sends automated reminders via both channels. The message includes the appointment date, time, clinic address, and a link to confirm, reschedule, or cancel. No phone call required. No staff time consumed.

The timing matters too. A reminder sent 24 hours before the appointment gives patients enough notice to adjust their schedule. A follow-up reminder 2 hours before catches last-minute forgetfulness.

## Let Patients Reschedule Without Calling You

The biggest barrier to rescheduling is friction. If a patient has to call the clinic, wait on hold, explain to the front desk, and negotiate a new slot, many will simply skip the appointment.

Doxxy's online booking portal lets patients reschedule themselves. They see real-time availability, pick a new slot, and get instant confirmation. The original slot opens up and gets filled from the waitlist — automatically.

This is the silent killer of no-shows. Patients who can reschedule in 30 seconds on their phone will do so. Patients who have to call during clinic hours while at work will ghost you.

## The Waitlist That Works While You're Busy

Cancellations create a race condition. The slot opens. Someone needs to fill it — fast. If a human has to call down a waitlist, the slot often goes unfilled because the process is too slow.

Doxxy's waitlist management is automatic. When a cancellation comes in, the system checks the waitlist, finds the best match based on appointment type and provider, and sends an offer. The first patient to accept gets the slot. No staff involvement. No empty chair.
