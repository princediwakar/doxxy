At month-end, they found INR 4,200 in unaccounted discrepancies — not theft, just honest counting errors across 700 cash transactions.

India processed over 130 billion UPI transactions in 2025. Your patients already pay for groceries, electricity, and chai with their phones. They're ready to pay your clinic the same way. The question is whether your clinic is ready to accept it.

## Why Patients Want to Pay Digitally

Convenience is the obvious answer. Nobody wants to stop at an ATM before a doctor's appointment. But there's more to it.

Digital payments create a transaction record. Patients can track healthcare spending for reimbursement, insurance claims, and tax filing. Cash receipts get lost. Digital receipts stay in the patient's email and SMS history forever.

Digital payments also feel more professional. A clinic that sends a payment link via WhatsApp and generates an itemized digital invoice signals competence. A clinic that only takes cash and hands you a handwritten receipt feels like it's stuck in 1995.

## What Digital Payments Mean for Your Clinic

**Faster collections.** Patients pay immediately when they receive a payment link — often before they leave the consultation room. No more "I'll pay next time" or running tabs.

**Zero reconciliation errors.** Every digital payment is tracked, timestamped, and matched to the patient and consultation automatically. The end-of-day report is always accurate.

**Better cash flow visibility.** You can see collections in real time. Who paid, who hasn't, what's pending — all in one dashboard. No more waiting for month-end to understand your revenue.

**Reduced theft and loss risk.** Less cash in the clinic means less risk of theft, misplacement, or "borrowing" from the register. Every rupee is accounted for.

## The Channels That Matter

UPI is non-negotiable. With over 300 million active users in India, it's how the majority of your patients prefer to pay. Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm — all UPI apps work with a standard QR code or payment link.

Cards still matter for older patients and corporate employees with health savings accounts. Net banking covers the rest.

Doxxy integrates with Razorpay to support UPI, credit and debit cards, and net banking — all in one payment flow. Patients get a payment link via SMS or WhatsApp after their consultation. They tap, pay, and get a digital receipt instantly.

## The Trust Factor

Some clinic owners worry that digital payments feel impersonal. The opposite is true. An itemized digital invoice with clear charges builds more trust than a verbal "that'll be five hundred rupees" without a receipt.

When patients can see exactly what they're paying for — consultation fee, procedure cost, medication — and pay with a method they trust, they're more likely to pay promptly and return.
