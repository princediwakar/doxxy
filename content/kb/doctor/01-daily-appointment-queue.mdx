## Prerequisites

- You must be logged in with a **Doctor** or **Superadmin** role in the active clinic.
- You must have a doctor profile set up (via Clinic Settings → Staff → Create Doctor Profile).
- Appointments must exist for today's date.

## The Goal

Navigate the daily schedule, see your patient queue organized by status, select a patient, and open their encounter canvas to begin or continue the consultation.

## Step-by-Step UI Instructions

### From the Schedule page (`/schedule`):

The page loads with today's date selected by default in the Date Carousel at the top.

### Understanding the Queue Layout

The left panel displays the patient queue, organized into three colour-coded sections:

1. **In Progress** (yellow dot) — Patients currently being seen or checked in. These are your active consultations.

2. **Waiting** (blue dot) — Patients with "Scheduled" status who have not yet been checked in or started.

3. **Completed** (green dot) — Patients whose visits are finished for the day. Their encounter is signed off.

Each section header shows the section name and the count of patients in it (e.g., "WAITING (5)").

### Selecting a Patient

4. Click on any patient's name in the queue. The patient row highlights with a ring/border to show it is selected.

5. The right-side detail panel opens, showing the **Encounter Canvas** with:
   - The **Patient Header** card with the patient's name, age, gender, MRN (Medical Record Number), department name, appointment time, and status badge.
   - The three-dot **more menu** (⋮) for additional actions (Schedule, Bill, Edit Patient, Edit Appointment).
   - The **Dictation Zone** (if the appointment is not yet completed and you can edit).
   - The **Inline Consultation Form** with specialty-specific sections.
   - The **Administrative Footer** with Bills and Previous Consultations accordions.

### Filtering by Doctor

6. Use the **Doctor filter dropdown** at the top of the Schedule page to switch between:
   - **"All Doctors"** — View every patient in the clinic today.
   - **"My Patients"** — View only patients assigned to you.

7. If you can't see a patient you expect, check the doctor filter — you may have "All Doctors" filtered to someone else, or you may be viewing only your own patients while the appointment is assigned to a colleague.

### Date Navigation

8. Use the **Date Carousel** at the top to switch between dates. Click left/right arrows or tap a specific date to view that day's queue.

9. Today's date is highlighted. Appointments from past and future dates are viewable but may have limited actions (e.g., you cannot edit a completed encounter from last week).

### Patient Row Information

Each patient row in the queue shows:
- **Patient name** and age.
- **Gender** (M/F).
- **Appointment time** (e.g., "10:30 AM").
- **Appointment type** badge (Walk-in or Digital).
- **Status** indicator via the section they appear in.

## Recommended Clinic Policy & Edge Cases

### Starting Your Day
1. Arrive at the clinic, log in to Doxxy, and navigate to the Schedule page.
2. Set the doctor filter to **"My Patients"** to see only your queue.
3. Review the Waiting section to understand your morning workload.
4. When ready for your first patient, ask the front desk to check them in. They appear in "In Progress".
5. Click the patient's name to open the encounter canvas and begin.

### Handling Walk-Ins Between Appointments
- Walk-in patients added by staff will appear in your queue immediately.
- If a walk-in is marked "In Progress", they are ready for you now. If "Waiting", the front desk has booked them but hasn't checked them in yet.
- Prioritize scheduled patients at their appointed time. Walk-ins can be seen between scheduled slots.

### Managing Your Queue Depth
- If your Waiting list is long and you're running behind, inform the front desk so they can update waiting patients.
- Use the queue counts to gauge your pace. A growing In Progress list with no movement to Completed means patients are waiting in rooms.

### Viewing Past Dates
- You can navigate to past dates to review completed consultations. The Date Carousel allows this, and clicking a patient opens their completed encounter in read-only mode (unless you have edit permissions).

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | What to do |
|---|---|
| **Queue is empty or shows no patients** | Check the Date Carousel — you may be on a date with no appointments. Switch to today. Also check the doctor filter — "My Patients" may show nothing if no patients are assigned to you today. |
| **Patient I'm seeing isn't in "In Progress"** | The front desk may not have checked them in yet. Ask them to click "Check In / Assign Room". |
| **Cannot see a colleague's patient** | Set the doctor filter to "All Doctors" to view the full clinic queue. |
| **Encounter canvas shows a loading skeleton** | Patient detail is still loading from the server. Wait a moment. If it persists, refresh the page. |
| **"Completed" patients are still visible** | Yes — completed patients remain visible for the day so you can review or re-open their notes if needed. |
| **Date Carousel not responding** | The dates are generated client-side. Check that the component loaded. Try refreshing the page. |
