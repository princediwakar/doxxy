## Prerequisites

- You must be logged in with a **Superadmin** role in the active clinic.
- A clinic must be selected.
- The clinic uses Doxxy's credit-based billing system.

## The Goal

Monitor the clinic's credit balance, review the transaction history (credit purchases and bill payments), understand credit usage, and purchase additional credit packs when needed.

## Step-by-Step UI Instructions

### From the Payments page (`/clinic/payments`):

The page header shows **"Payments"** with the subtitle *"Credits and transaction history"* and an **"Add Credits"** button.

### Understanding the Stats Cards

Three cards sit at the top:

1. **Available Balance:** Shows the current credit balance. Displayed in green when healthy (10+ credits), in red when low (below 10 credits).

2. **Total Usage:** The total number of credits consumed (appointments billed). Each appointment consumes credits from the balance.

3. **Account Status:** A badge showing either **"Active"** (green) when credits are available or **"Low Credits"** (red/destructive) when the balance reaches zero.

### Credit Usage Progress Bar

If credits have been purchased, a progress bar shows:
- The percentage of total purchased credits that have been used.
- The bar is blue under 90% usage, orange when above 90% (warning zone).
- Below the bar: "X used of Y total purchased."

### Transaction History

The **"Transaction History"** card lists all payment transactions, newest first. Each row shows:

- **Status Icon:** Green checkmark for completed, red X for failed, yellow clock for pending.
- **Transaction Type:** "Credit Top-up" or "Bill Payment".
- **Date & Time:** Formatted as "MMM dd, yyyy • h:mm AM/PM".
- **Credits:** For top-ups, a blue badge showing "+X credits".
- **Amount:** The transaction amount in rupees (₹).
- **Status Badge:** Coloured badge — green "completed", red "failed", yellow "pending".

If there are no transactions, an empty state shows: *"No transactions found."* with a link to purchase the first credit pack.

### Purchasing Credits

5. Click the **"Add Credits"** button (top-right, blue).

6. The **Credit Purchase Modal** opens. Select a credit pack and complete the payment.

7. After a successful purchase, the balance updates and the transaction appears in the history.

## Recommended Clinic Policy & Edge Cases

### Monitoring Credit Levels
- Set a personal threshold (e.g., 20 credits) at which you purchase more. Running out of credits during clinic hours disrupts the billing workflow.
- The "Low Credits" status appears when the balance is 0, but plan ahead — purchase before hitting zero.

### Understanding Credit Consumption
- Each completed bill/consultation typically consumes 1 credit from the balance.
- The "Total Usage" number equals the number of billed consultations processed through Doxxy.
- Monitor the usage rate: if you go through 50 credits per week, plan to purchase packs that cover at least 2-4 weeks.

### Transaction Record Keeping
- All transactions (both credit purchases and bill payments) are permanently recorded. Use the transaction history for reconciliation with your accounting records.
- Failed transactions are logged and visible — investigate any red "failed" entries to ensure no revenue is lost.

### Credit Packs and Pricing
- Credit packs come in fixed bundles. The per-credit cost decreases with larger packs.
- Purchased credits do not expire. They remain available until consumed.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | What to do |
|---|---|
| **Balance shows 0 but you recently purchased** | Refresh the page. If still 0, check the transaction history for a "failed" status on your recent purchase. |
| **"Low Credits" badge showing** | Purchase credits immediately to avoid interruption. Click "Add Credits". |
| **Transaction shows "pending" status** | Some payment methods take time to confirm. Wait a few minutes and refresh. If pending for more than 1 hour, contact Doxxy support. |
| **"Failed" transaction in history** | The payment did not go through. Try purchasing again. If it fails repeatedly, check with your payment provider or bank. |
| **Credit Purchase Modal does not open** | Check for browser pop-up blockers. Ensure your internet connection is stable. |
| **Usage seems higher than expected** | Each completed bill consumes a credit. Check if any bills were generated for cancelled or no-show appointments, which may indicate billing process issues. |
