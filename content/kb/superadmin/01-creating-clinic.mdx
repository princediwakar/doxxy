## Prerequisites

- You must be logged in to Doxxy. If this is your first time using Doxxy after signup, you will not yet belong to any clinic.
- You should know the basic details of the clinic you want to create: name, address, contact information.

## The Goal

Create a new clinic in Doxxy and set yourself up as either a practicing doctor or an administrator-only superadmin. Once created, the clinic becomes your active workspace and you can begin inviting staff, adding patients, and scheduling appointments.

## Step-by-Step UI Instructions

### From the Create Clinic page (`/create-clinic`):

The wizard has three steps, shown at the top of the form: **1. Clinic Details → 2. Departments → 3. Your Role**.

### Step 1: Clinic Details

1. **Enter the Clinic Name** in the first field (e.g., "Neurovision"). This is required and must be at least 2 characters.

2. **Fill in the Clinic Address** (optional but recommended). Enter the full street address (e.g., "123, Connaught Place, New Delhi").

3. **Enter the Clinic Email** (optional). Use a valid email format (e.g., "email@clinic.com"). This is the clinic's public-facing contact email.

4. **Enter the Clinic Phone** (optional). The clinic's primary contact number (e.g., "9876543210").

5. **Enter the Website** (optional). You can enter a full URL (e.g., "https://www.clinic.com") or just a domain (e.g., "www.clinic.com").

6. Click **"Next: Select Departments"**. The form validates your inputs and advances to Step 2.

### Step 2: Departments

7. **Select the departments your clinic offers.** A list of available department types loads from the system (e.g., Cardiology, Neurology, Ophthalmology, Dental, General Medicine). Each department appears with a checkbox.

8. Check the box next to each department your clinic will operate. You must select at least one department.

   - If the department list fails to load, you will see an error message with a **"Try Again"** button to reload.
   - If no department types exist in the system, you will see "No departments found."

9. Click **"Next: Your Role"** to proceed, or **"Back"** to return to clinic details.

### Step 3: Your Role

10. **Choose whether you are a practicing doctor or an administrator only:**
    - Select **"Yes, I'm a practicing doctor"** if you will see patients and appear in appointment lists. This opens additional fields.
    - Select **"No, I'm an administrator only"** if you will manage the clinic but not see patients.

11. **If you selected "Yes, I'm a practicing doctor":**
    - **Primary Department (required):** Choose your primary department from the dropdown. Only departments you selected in Step 2 appear here.
    - **Consultation Fee (₹):** Enter your consultation fee in rupees (e.g., 500). You can adjust this later from your profile.

12. Click **"Create Clinic"**. The button shows "Creating Clinic..." while the server action runs.

13. On success:
    - A green toast notification confirms: *"Clinic [name] created successfully."*
    - You are automatically redirected to the Schedule page (`/schedule`) with the new clinic set as your active clinic.
    - Your role (superadmin or doctor) is assigned based on your selection.

14. On failure: A red toast shows the error message. Check the inputs and try again.

## Recommended Clinic Policy & Edge Cases

### Naming Your Clinic
- Use the clinic's legal or brand name consistently. This name appears on invoices, prescriptions, and patient-facing documents.
- Avoid special characters that may cause issues in printed documents.

### Department Selection Strategy
- Select only the departments you actively operate. You can add or remove departments later from Clinic Settings → Departments.
- If a department type you need is not in the list, contact Doxxy support to have it added to the system's department catalog.

### Administrator-Only vs. Doctor
- If you select "No, I'm an administrator only," you will have full superadmin privileges but will NOT appear in the doctor selection dropdown when booking appointments.
- You can create a separate doctor profile later via Clinic Settings → Staff if you decide to practice later.
- This choice cannot be undone from this wizard, but a superadmin can always have a doctor profile created for them by another superadmin.

### Multiple Clinics
- If your organization manages multiple clinic locations, repeat this process for each location. Use the Clinic Switcher in the top navigation to move between clinics.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | What to do |
|---|---|
| **"Clinic name must be at least 2 characters" error** | The name field is empty or too short. Enter the full clinic name. |
| **"Invalid email address" error** | The email field contains an improperly formatted email. Correct it or leave it empty. |
| **"Select at least one department" error** | You must check at least one department checkbox before proceeding. |
| **"Select at least one department when registering as a doctor" error** | In Step 3, you selected "Yes, I'm a practicing doctor" but did not pick your primary department from the dropdown. |
| **Department list is empty or shows an error** | Click "Try Again" to reload. If the problem persists, check your internet connection. |
| **"Creating Clinic..." hangs indefinitely** | The server action may have timed out. Refresh the page and try again. Your clinic details from Step 1 are preserved in the form. |
| **Redirected to wrong page after creation** | You should land on `/schedule`. If redirected elsewhere, manually navigate to `/schedule`. |
