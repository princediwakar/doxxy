## Prerequisites

- You must be logged in with a **Superadmin** role in the active clinic.
- A clinic must be selected.
- Department types must exist in the Doxxy system catalog. If a needed specialty is missing, contact support.

## The Goal

Activate the departments your clinic offers by adding them from the system catalog. Only activated departments appear in doctor profiles, appointment booking, and consultation forms.

## Step-by-Step UI Instructions

### From the Departments page (`/clinic/departments`):

The page header shows **"Departments"** with the subtitle *"Manage clinic departments"* and two badges: the number of **Active** departments and the number of **Available** (not yet activated) departments.

### Viewing Departments

The page is split into two sections:

1. **Active Departments** (top section, marked with a green checkmark icon): These departments are currently active in your clinic. Each card shows:
   - A department-specific icon (brain for Neurology, eye for Ophthalmology, heart for Cardiology, smile for Dental, stethoscope for General Medicine/Internal Medicine, activity pulse for others).
   - The department name and an **"Active"** badge.
   - An **X button** to remove/deactivate the department.

2. **Available Departments** (bottom section, marked with a + icon): These are system-level department types not yet active in your clinic. Each card shows:
   - A department icon.
   - The department name and an **"Available"** badge.
   - A **+ button** to add/activate the department.

### Adding a Department

3. Locate the department you want to add in the **Available Departments** section.

4. Use the **search bar** at the top if you have many departments — type part of the name (e.g., "cardio") to filter.

5. Click the **+ button** on the department card. A green toast confirms: *"Department added successfully"*.

6. The department card moves from "Available" to "Active".

### Removing a Department

7. Locate the department in the **Active Departments** section.

8. Click the **X button** on the department card. A toast confirms: *"Department removed successfully"*.

9. The department card moves from "Active" back to "Available".

   - Removing a department does NOT delete historical data. Existing consultations and doctor assignments linked to this department remain intact.
   - Doctors assigned to the removed department will need to be reassigned via the Staff page.

## Recommended Clinic Policy & Edge Cases

### Department Naming and Icons
- Departments are system-defined types, not custom names. You cannot rename them or upload custom icons.
- If your clinic uses a specialty not in the list (e.g., Rheumatology, Nephrology), contact Doxxy support to request its addition.

### Activating Only What You Operate
- Activate only departments you actively offer. Extraneous departments clutter the doctor assignment dropdowns and appointment booking doctor lists.
- Multi-specialty clinics should activate all relevant departments so each doctor can be assigned correctly.

### When Removing a Department
- Before removing a department, check if any doctors are assigned to it (Staff page → filter by role "Doctor").
- Reassign those doctors to a different department first. Removing a department does not automatically reassign its doctors.
- If you accidentally remove a department, simply re-add it — the toggle is immediate.

### Regional and Specialty Considerations
- Some department types may be more common in certain regions. The system catalog covers a broad range of medical specialties.
- A "General Medicine" or "Internal Medicine" department is recommended as a catch-all for general practitioners.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | What to do |
|---|---|
| **"Access Denied" message** | You are not a superadmin in this clinic. Only superadmins can manage departments. |
| **"Failed to add department" toast** | The server action failed. Check your internet connection and try clicking the + button again. |
| **"Failed to remove department" error** | The server action failed. Try again. |
| **Department not appearing in the list** | Use the search bar to filter. If still not found, the department type may not exist in the system catalog. Contact support. |
| **"No departments match your search"** | Try different search terms. The search matches against department names. |
| **"No departments found" (both sections empty)** | No department types exist in the system. Contact Doxxy support. |
| **Removed department still shows for doctors** | Doctor department assignments are separate from clinic department activation. Update the doctor's department from the Staff page. |
