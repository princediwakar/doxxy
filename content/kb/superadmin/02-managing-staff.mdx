## Prerequisites

- You must be logged in with a **Superadmin** role in the active clinic.
- You must have at least one clinic created and selected.
- You need the invitee's email address and name. A phone number is recommended but optional.

## The Goal

Add members to your clinic by inviting them via email, assign them appropriate roles and departments, and manage their access over time — including removing members, changing roles, and creating doctor profiles.

## Step-by-Step UI Instructions

### From the Staff page (`/clinic/staff`):

The page displays a header with the member count badge, a search bar, a role filter dropdown, and the member list.

### Inviting a New Member

1. Click the **"Invite"** button (top-right, with a + icon).

2. The **"Invite New Member"** dialog opens with fields: Name, Email, Phone (optional), and a Role dropdown.

3. **Fill in the invitee's details:**
   - **Name:** The person's full name.
   - **Email:** Must be a valid email address. This is where the invitation is sent.
   - **Phone:** Optional. Recommended for staff who will interact with patients.
   - **Role:** Select from the dropdown — **Staff**, **Doctor**, or **Superadmin**.

4. Click **"Send Invite"**. A success toast appears.

5. If the email delivery fails, an extended toast notification shows the invitation link. Click **"Copy Link"** to copy it and share it manually with the invitee (e.g., via WhatsApp).

### Creating a Doctor Profile for an Existing Member

6. Find the member in the list. Each member card shows their name, email, role badge, and department.

7. Click the **stethoscope icon button** on the member's card. The **"Create Doctor Profile"** dialog opens.

8. Fill in:
   - **Name** and **Email** (pre-filled from the member's profile).
   - **Primary Specialization:** e.g., "General Physician", "Cardiologist".
   - **Consultation Fee (₹):** The doctor's per-visit fee.
   - **Bio:** A short professional bio.
   - **Department:** Select from the clinic's active departments.

9. Click **"Create Doctor Profile"**. A success toast confirms the profile was created. The doctor now appears in appointment booking lists.

### Editing a Member's Role or Department

10. Click the **pencil/edit icon** on the member's card. The **"Edit Member"** dialog opens.

11. Change the **Role** dropdown and/or **Department** dropdown as needed.
    - If you change the role to **Staff**, the department is automatically cleared (since departments are for doctors).

12. Click **"Update Member"**. The member's access is updated immediately.

### Removing a Member

13. Click the **trash/delete icon** on the member's card.

14. The member is removed from the clinic. Their user account is not deleted — they simply lose access to this clinic. They can be re-invited later.

### Searching and Filtering

15. Use the **Search members...** input at the top to filter by name or email. The list updates as you type.

16. Use the **Role filter dropdown** (default "All Roles") to show only Superadmins, Doctors, or Staff.

## Recommended Clinic Policy & Edge Cases

### Role Assignment Guidelines
- **Superadmin:** Grant only to clinic owners or senior administrators. Superadmins can manage members, departments, clinic details, financials, and all sensitive data.
- **Doctor:** Assign to any medical professional who sees patients. They need a doctor profile to appear in appointment lists.
- **Staff:** Assign to front desk, billing, pharmacy, and administrative personnel. Staff can book appointments, check in patients, manage billing, and view the pharmacy.

### Invitation Expiry
- Invitation links expire after a set period (determined by Supabase Auth). If an invitee hasn't accepted within 24 hours, consider resending the invitation by removing and re-inviting them.

### Offboarding a Member
- When a doctor or staff member leaves the clinic:
  1. Remove them from the clinic via the delete button on the Staff page.
  2. Their historical data (consultations, bills, appointments) remains intact for audit purposes.
  3. If the departing member was a doctor, any future appointments assigned to them should be reassigned to another doctor.

### Multiple Clinics
- A single user account can be a member of multiple clinics with different roles in each. Removing them from one clinic does not affect their membership in others.

### Department Assignment for Doctors
- A doctor can be assigned to only one department at a time. If a doctor practices across multiple specialties, assign them to their primary department and note the others in their bio.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | What to do |
|---|---|
| **"Email and name are required" error** | Both Name and Email fields must be filled before sending the invite. |
| **"Invalid email format" error** | The email address is malformed. Check for typos (e.g., missing @ or domain). |
| **"Failed to invite member" error** | The Supabase Edge Function may have failed. Check your internet connection and try again. If persistent, use the manual link sharing fallback. |
| **Invitee did not receive the email** | Check the toast message — if it shows an invitation link, the email delivery failed. Copy the link and share it manually. Ask the invitee to check their spam folder. |
| **"Failed to create doctor profile" error** | Ensure all required fields are filled (Name, Email). The member must already exist in the clinic. |
| **"Failed to update member" error** | Network issue or server action failure. Try again. |
| **"Access Denied" message shown** | You are not a superadmin in this clinic. Only superadmins can manage members. |
| **Member list is empty after inviting** | The page data may be cached. Refresh the page. The new member appears once they accept the invitation and sign in. |
| **Cannot remove the last superadmin** | Every clinic must have at least one superadmin. Promote another member to superadmin before removing yourself. |
